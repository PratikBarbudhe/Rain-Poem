Rain Rain

Rain, rain, come come again,
Washing away the dust and pain.
From the heavens, you gently fall,
Refreshing the earth, one and all.

Pitter-patter, your rhythm plays,
Serenading nature in myriad ways.
Leaves glisten as you softly touch,
A symphony of droplets, we love so much.

Rain, rain, nourish the land,
Sowing seeds with a tender hand.
The flowers bloom, their colors bright,
Under your touch, the world feels right.

Let your gentle showers cleanse the air,
And wash away every worry and care.
Rain, rain, a gift from above,
We welcome you with hearts full of love.
